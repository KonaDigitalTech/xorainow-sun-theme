From the Edge of Infrastructure to the Frontier of Intelligence

Xakti Futuristic is an AI-first technology company from Guwahati, India — building the hardware, software, and talent that powers real-world AI.

Our Story

Xakti Futuristic was founded on a simple conviction: the next wave of AI isn't in the cloud. It's at the edge, in the physical world, embedded in the machines and vehicles and campuses that surround us. Incorporated in February 2025, Xakti builds from India for the world — creating intelligent systems that work in the most constrained, challenging, and critical environments on earth.

Mission, Vision & Philosophy

Mission

To democratize advanced AI safety and automation technology — making intelligent, edge-native systems accessible in constrained environments across fleet, campus, industry, and enterprise.

Vision

To become the leading Physical AI company from India — where every vehicle, campus, and enterprise workflow is guided by intelligent, proactive AI systems.

Philosophy

"Because We XF." — A commitment to human safety, data privacy, environmental sustainability, and AI that works where it matters most.
