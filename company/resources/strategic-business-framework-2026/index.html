1. Strategic Intent

XaktiNow exists to close the gap between AI ambition and enterprise execution. While the market overflows with AI hype and ServiceNow generalists, XaktiNow combines deep platform mastery with AI-native delivery to help organizations move from pilot to production - fast, governed, and at scale.

This framework defines how each of XaktiNow's seven business units operates, how they interlock, and how leadership should allocate resources, measure progress, and make tradeoffs across the portfolio.

2. The Seven-Pillar Operating Model

XaktiNow's business is organized into seven distinct but interconnected pillars. Each pillar has its own P&L accountability, talent requirements, delivery model, and growth trajectory - but all share a common client relationship layer and brand identity.
