XF Classroom — Intelligence for Better Teaching

AI that helps educators understand engagement, automate attendance, and improve learning outcomes — without disrupting the classroom.

See XF Classroom Demo Talk to Education Team

The Challenge

Faculty lack real-time feedback on student engagement. Attendance tracking wastes 10-15 minutes per class. Traditional surveillance violates student privacy and FERPA compliance.

Core Features

Attention Heatmaps

Per-student gaze tracking generating real-time "Engagement Scores" for instructors. Identify students who need support.

Automated Attendance

Facial recognition at entry/exit — zero admin overhead, fully accurate. Saves 10-15 minutes per class.

Teaching Effectiveness Analytics

Correlate attention patterns with teaching styles to identify what works. Data-driven pedagogy improvement.

Student Wellbeing Flags

Detect distress signals for early intervention by counselors. Privacy-preserving mental health support.

Privacy-First Design

All processing happens on-device. No raw video stored or transmitted. No facial images or biometric templates stored. FERPA, GDPR, and DPDP Act compliant.
